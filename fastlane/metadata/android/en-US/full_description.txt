Lab+ for Gitlab is your unofficial Companion for Gitlab.
Use it to view your Repositories and interact with Issues, Merge Requests and Source Code

<b>Features:</b>
<ul>
 <li>View your Repositories</li>
 <li>View and edit your Issues</li>
 <li>View and edit your Merge Requests</li>
 <li>View your Projects Pipeline Status</li>
 <li>View your Projects Code</li>
</ul>
